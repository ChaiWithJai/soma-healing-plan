Week 1

Foundation - "Essence of Breath"

20-25 minutes daily

Build the foundation of conscious breathing. Your body has been in survival mode - it's time to switch to healing mode through parasympathetic activation.

Day 1-2: Breath Awareness

Morning (10 min)
- Measure MBHT immediately upon waking
- Record in phone notes with date
- 5 min diaphragmatic breathing (lying down)
- Set intention: "My knee is healing"

Day 3-4: Rhythmic Breathing

Morning (15 min)
- MBHT measurement
- 10 min Reduced Breathing Exercise
- Use SOMA breath entrainment music

Day 5-7: Heart Coherence

Morning (15 min)
- MBHT measurement
- 5-5 breathing (5 sec in, 5 sec out) for 10 min

  • Target MBHT: 25-35 seconds (baseline)
Week 2

Movement + Stress Relief - "Rhythm of Calm"

25-30 minutes daily

Add gentle movement to release the psoas (connected to knee/hip chain) and master the anxiety-busting 2:4 breathing pattern.

Day 8-10: Modified Hindu Squats

Morning (20 min)
- MBHT measurement
- 5 min diaphragmatic warm-up
- 10 Modified Hindu Squats
- 5 min 2:4 breathing cool-down

Modified Hindu Squat (Knee-Safe)

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Arms extended forward for balance
  3. Descend SLOWLY - only go as deep as comfortable
  4. Keep knees tracking over toes (not collapsing)
  5. Rise on exhale, descend on inhale
  6. Focus on PSOAS release, not depth
  7. Stop if any sharp knee pain

Day 11-12: Fear Control Practice

Evening - Surgery Anxiety Protocol (10 min)
- 2:4 breathing for 5-10 min
- When fear arises, switch to 2:4
- "I breathe in calm (2 sec), I release fear (4 sec)"

Week 2 Milestones

  • Can do 15 Hindu Squats without knee pain
  • 2:4 breathing feels automatic when stressed
  • MBHT improved 5-10 seconds from Week 1
  • Target MBHT: 35-45 seconds

30-35 minutes daily

Color Breathing Knee Protocol

  1. Lie down, close eyes
  2. Begin rhythmic breathing (4-4 pattern)
  3. Visualize healing GREEN or BLUE light
  4. On inhale: Light enters through top of head
  5. On retention: Direct light to your LEFT KNEE
  6. Visualize light wrapping around MCL
  7. See ligament fibers knitting back together
  8. On exhale: Release inflammation as gray smoke
  9. Repeat for 15-20 minutes

MCL Healing Mind Movie

  5. Feel the emotions of being fully mobile
  6. Zoom into the knee - see healthy MCL fibers
  7. Add triumphant music in your mind
  8. Step INTO the movie - become the healed version

Key Concepts to Master

  1. Parasympathetic activation: Slow breathing activates "rest & digest" mode - essential for tissue repair
  2. Psoas-knee connection: The psoas connects to hip flexion; chronic tension pulls the hip, affecting knee alignment and MCL stress
